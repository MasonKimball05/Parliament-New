Executive Board

All 9 Executive Officers

Permanent Committee

Composition: All 9 Executive Board Officers (President, Executive Vice President & IFC Delegate, Vice President of Administration, Vice President of Brotherhood, Vice President of Finance, Vice President of Risk Management, Vice President of Recruitment, Vice President of Education, Vice President of Programming)

KC

Kai Committee

5-9 members including elected delegates

Judicial

Composition: Kai Committee Chair (Vice President of Brotherhood), plus 1-2 delegates from each class (Senior, Junior, Sophomore, At-large), totaling 5-9 members

Responsibilities (per Bylaws Article VII): Handles all disciplinary reviews, maintains written records, ensures Rights of the Accused are upheld

BC

Brotherhood Committee

Social bonding and chapter culture

Responsibilities (per Bylaws Article VII): Organizes brotherhood events, ensures positive atmosphere

Source: Constitution Article V, Bylaws Article VII

RC

Recruitment Committee

Membership growth and rush activities

Composition (per Bylaws Article VII): 5-9 members plus Vice President of Recruitment and President

Voting: Supermajority vote required for bids

Source: Constitution Article V, Bylaws Article VII

EC

Education Committee

New member orientation and education

Responsibilities (per Bylaws Article VII): Standard New Member Orientation, annual education, member development

Source: Constitution Article V, Bylaws Article VII

RM

Risk Management Committee

Safety and policy compliance

Responsibilities (per Bylaws Article VII): Ensures Beta Risk Management Policy implementation, member education

Source: Constitution Article V, Bylaws Article VII

FC

Finance Committee

Budget management and financial planning

Responsibilities (per Bylaws Article VII): Budget preparation, bill collection, complete financial records

Source: Constitution Article V, Bylaws Article VII

AC

Administration Committee

Communications and records

Responsibilities (per Bylaws Article VII): Announcements, reporting, alumni relations, social media

Source: Constitution Article V, Bylaws Article VII

PC

Programming Committee

Events and activities

Responsibilities (per Bylaws Article VII): Service, philanthropy, intramurals, special events

Source: Constitution Article V, Bylaws Article VII

RT

Ritual Committee

Ceremonial traditions and rituals

Composition (per Bylaws Article VII): President, Vice President of Brotherhood, Vice President of Education, Ritual Chair, Chorister, plus 2 additional members

Source: Constitution Article V, Bylaws Article VII

CB

Constitution and Bylaws Committee

Governing documents and amendments

Composition (per Bylaws Article VII): Minimum 5 members

Responsibilities: Drafts amendments

Voting: Requires unanimous committee vote to forward amendments

Source: Constitution Article V, Bylaws Article VII
