Advisor Selection & Appointments

Selection Criteria

  • Must be initiated member of Beta Theta Pi
  • Strong understanding of fraternity values and policies
  • Availability to commit time and energy to chapter
  • Relevant expertise in assigned area (recruitment, academics, etc.)
  • Positive relationship with undergraduate members
  • Good standing with General Fraternity

Appointment Process

  1. Chapter identifies need for advisor or replacement
  2. Recommendations solicited from members and alumni
  3. President interviews potential advisors
  4. Executive Board votes on appointment
  5. Appointment reported to General Fraternity for approval
  6. Formal installation and orientation for new advisor
